Is renewable energy the solution of decarbonizing? Outlook in renewable potential and performance

Aim

Climate change is a formidable environmental concern, which leads to natural calamities and biodiversity loss, bringing famine, health risks and poverty to human-being, whose chief culprit is carbon emission. Fossil fuels are the largest contributor to carbon emission, which accounts for over 90% of CO2 (The UN, n.d.). Therefore, renewable energy, which is sustainable, clean and reliable is the key to the solution. This project aims to provide an outlook on the potential and performance of renewable energy by using interactive graphs.

Potential & Performance

This graph demonstrates that fossil fuel is dominant in electricity production, but the percentage of electricity production by renewable energy increased from 2000. Among different renewable energy, hydropower is the main source. Moreover, wind energy and solar energy are faster growing, which has great potential. For data, it is from Our World in Data downloaded in a CSV file. The data set is massive across different countries and times, so it is hard to select manually. The key to energy transition is the price of the energy, lower renewable costs make them more competitive compared to fossil energy. The line chart shows the overall decrease in the cost of renewable energy. Moreover, renewable energy technologies follow learning curves, and fossil fuels do not, so renewable energy may be much cheaper than fossil fuels in the future (Roser, 2020). Data from the IRENA was downloaded by a CSV file.

The driver in the price change in renewable energy is technology (Roser, 2020) The number of patents filed by solar technologies has the largest share, which has great potential. Data was downloaded from the Our World in Data in CSV file and was cleaned by Python (Colab Notes).

The graph shows that investment in renewable energy increased from 2004 to 2018. solar technology and wind technology have a large share of investment which is consistent with the good performance of cost decline in renewable energy. Data is from the Our World in Data

Every dollar of investment in renewable energy generates three times more jobs than in the fossil fuel industry (The UN, 2022). In this graph, many jobs are created by renewable technology. Solar technology creates a third of the jobs of the total renewable technology, proving its potential again. It can be argued that renewable energy stimulates employment, bringing economic benefits. Data from the IRENA was downloaded in a CSV file.

Conclusion

Renewable energy is taking an increasing proportion of the energy structure, and its cost has decreased over the past 10 years which implies that it has a stronger competitive compared to fossil energy. The increasing number of patents filed by renewable energy shows the progress of technology. Moreover, the investment also continuously increases. These indicators also have internal connections. Among renewable energy, solar energy and wind energy have the most outstanding performance. Finally, low cost, economic benefit and technological progress make renewable energy affordable, sustainable and safe. This provides motivation to the countries to reduce carbon emissions since they can still develop their economies in a sustainable way.
